1) Scope

This Policy applies to the Torche mobile application and any in-app features. It does not apply to third-party services you may access through the App (for example, app stores or payment processors). Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

2) Personal information we collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from users.

Because we do not collect personal data, we do not sell, share, or disclose personal data to third parties.

3) Device permissions (Camera/Flashlight)

To provide core functionality, the App requests permission to use the device’s camera hardware solely to control the torch/flashlight.

  • Purpose: Turning the device’s flashlight on or off.
  • No image/video access: The App does not take photos, record video, capture, store, or transmit any imagery or audio.
  • Control: You can grant or revoke the camera/flashlight permission at any time via your device settings. Without this permission, the flashlight feature will not work.

4) Payments & processors

The App may offer in-app purchases or redirect you to a payment processor to complete a transaction. We do not collect or store any personal or financial information associated with your payment.

  • Handled by third parties: Payment details (e.g., name, email, card or UPI information, billing address) are processed directly by the app store or payment processor under their own privacy policies.
  • What we receive: We may receive non-identifying confirmations (e.g., a purchase success signal or a non-personal transaction identifier) needed to unlock features. These confirmations do not identify you.

Please review the privacy policy of your app store or the payment processor for details on how they handle your information.

5) Analytics, tracking, ads, and emails

  • Analytics/Tracking: No. We do not use analytics SDKs or tracking technologies (including Google Analytics).
  • Advertising: No. We do not display ads.
  • Emails: No. We do not send marketing or transactional emails.
  • Cookies/online identifiers: No. We do not use cookies or collect device identifiers.

6) Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7) Your privacy rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, applicable data protection laws (e.g., GDPR/UK GDPR) provide rights such as access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability, and the right to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority.

Note: We do not collect personal data. If you contact us to exercise a right, we will explain that we do not hold personal data about you in the App. If you made a purchase, your app store or payment processor may hold personal information; please contact them directly for data-rights requests.

Legal bases: Because we do not process personal data, we do not rely on GDPR legal bases for the App’s operation. Where third-party processors (e.g., app stores) process your data, they determine their own lawful bases.

8) Your privacy rights (CCPA/CPRA – California)

For California residents, the California Consumer Privacy Act (as amended by the CPRA) grants rights to know, access, delete, correct, and opt-out of sale or sharing of personal information, and to be free from discrimination for exercising these rights.

  • No sale or sharing: We do not sell or share personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA.
  • Right to know/access/delete/correct: We do not collect personal information in the App. If you submit such a request, we will respond explaining that we have no personal information about you.
  • Sensitive personal information: We do not collect it.

To submit a request, see Contact us below. We will verify your request consistent with the law and our data-minimization practices.

9) CalOPPA & Do-Not-Track

California Online Privacy Protection Act (CalOPPA) requires disclosures about “Do-Not-Track” (DNT) signals. We do not track users over time or across third-party websites, so the App does not respond to DNT signals.

10) Security & retention

  • Security: We design the App to minimize data collection. Because we do not collect personal information, typical data breach risks are reduced.
  • Retention: We do not retain personal data. Non-identifying purchase confirmations may be cached locally to enable features; these do not identify you and can be cleared by reinstalling the App or clearing app data.

11) International users & transfers

The App is developed in India (State: Andhra Pradesh). We do not collect or transfer personal data via the App. If you interact with third-party processors (e.g., app stores, payment providers), your information may be processed in other jurisdictions according to their policies.

12) Changes to this policy

We may update this Policy to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. We will revise the “Effective date” above and, where appropriate, provide in-app notice. Your continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised Policy.
